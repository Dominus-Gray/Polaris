## Pricing Options

### Monthly Billing
- Standard monthly rates as listed above
- No commitment required
- Change tiers anytime

### Annual Billing (Save 20%)
- **Starter**: $1,192/year (save $596)
- **Professional**: $2,392/year (save $1,196) 
- **Enterprise**: $4,792/year (save $2,396)
- **Enterprise Plus**: $7,992/year (save $3,996)

### Volume Discounts
- **5+ agencies**: 10% additional discount
- **10+ agencies**: 15% additional discount
- **State-wide programs**: Custom enterprise pricing

### Additional Client Capacity
- **Starter**: $2 per additional client/month
- **Professional**: $1.50 per additional client/month
- **Enterprise**: $1 per additional client/month

### Premium Support Add-ons
